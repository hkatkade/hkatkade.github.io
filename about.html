About us

WHO ARE WE?

Urban Research Foundation is a Non Profit Organization registered under Section 8 of Companies Act in January 2021. The organization is established with a mission to create a purposeful discourse around urbanization in small and medium cities in India. Through our work, we aim to bring innovative planning and design strategies to these cities. We work closely with local urban administrations, Civic organizations, NGOs as well and private corporations on various local urban issues. Realizing the huge gap in available data from smaller cities and its implication on the effective implementation of State and National Urban missions and programs, we invest our time in building essential data that can help cities make better decisions. Through engaging with citizens, we gain important insights about on-ground implications of urban practices.

What do we do?

Urban Design

Finding Innovative, cost effective, efficient, and contextual solutions to urban challenges

Urban Policies

Tackling various urban issues & informing urban growth via policy framework and guidelines

Citizen Engagement

Promoting participatory planning through awareness & engagement activities using vernacular language

Capacity Building

Knowledge and Capacity building of officials, NGOs and communities to enable effective governance

Data and Research

Data bank on Small and Medium cities with research through the lens of Regional and Urban Planning

Expert advisory

Technical Urban Planning, Research, and Design support from smaller to medium cities
