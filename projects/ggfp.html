Get Going Flyover Park


Description

Get Going is an NGO working in the field of Health and Fitness for women. With a common vision of creating gender sensitive public spaces in the city, URF has collaborated with Get Going to develop this space as a health and fitness park with a focus on women and child rights advocacy. The space is being designed with the principles of re-use and recycles. The design also prioritizes the needs of women, children and senior citizens while using a public space.
